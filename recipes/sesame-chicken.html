Sesame Chicken

Description

This dish is a perfect mixture of sweet and savory. Full of protien from the delicious chicken and carbs from the sauce coated white rice. Turn any average weekday dinner into a meal that leaves your friends or family saying "where did you buy this from? Its amazing!".

Ingredients

Sauce

    • 1/3 cup raw honey
      3 tablespoons tomato paste
      1 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
      1 tablespoons Sriracha sauce
      1 teaspoon sesame oil
      1/2 cup cold water
      1 tablespoon cornstarch
      1/2 teaspoon salt

    • chicken

    • 1 and 1/4 teaspoons salt, divided
      3/4 teaspoon garlic powder, divided
      1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch pieces
      1 egg
      3/4 cup all-purpose flour
      1/2 cup cold water
      1/2 cup cornstarch
      2 tablespoons avocado oil
      1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
      peanut oil for frying
      1/2 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds
