Baked Buttermilk Donuts

Description

This simple yet yummy donut recipe will leave you with crispy yet soft little miracles that we like to call buttermilk donuts. Mouth watering yet? After you finish making them you can feel free to add any type of glaze you prefer for your donuts!

ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable shortening, melted and cooled slightly
  • 1 cup sifted powdered sugar (Optional)
